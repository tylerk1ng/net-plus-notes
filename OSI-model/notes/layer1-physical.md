# Layer 1: Physical

**Function**: Transmits raw bit streams over physical medium (no addressing/errors).

**PDU**: Bits—converts L2 frame to electrical/light/radio signals.

**Key protocols/devices**: Cables (UTP Cat6, fiber), NICs, hubs, repeaters. RJ45, SFP.

**Encapsulation**: No header—frame becomes 1s/0s on wire (ends encapsulation).

**Common issues**: No link lights, cable damage, distance exceeded (100m Ethernet).

**Attack example**: Wiretapping

**Professor Messer notes**: 
- "Cables, connectors and signals only—no intelligence"

**THM notes**: 
- pure hardware
